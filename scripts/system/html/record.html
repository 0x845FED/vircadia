Recordings Being Played Unload

If you're in your own sandbox or are a domain administrator:

  • Right-click the High Fidelity Sandbox icon in your system tray and click “Settings”.
  • In the “Scripts” section add a new script URL, playRecordingAC.js, and set the number of instances to 1 or more.
  • Click “Save and restart”.
  • Now you can play recordings in the domain!
